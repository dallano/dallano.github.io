About Me

Hey there, I'm a software developer with a background in game design, a love for creative tools, and a soft spot for long-term growth. I've always been drawn to building things, especially with computers, and I've found a lot of joy (and challenge) in turning that into a career.

I've lived and traveled around the world, picking up languages, perspectives, and an appreciation for how people think and work differently. That global experience has shaped how I approach collaboration, learning, and problem-solving. Whether I'm diving into a new tech stack or helping a team ship a project, I bring that mindset with me.

I've worked on indie game projects, emulation servers, and tool development across Lua, C++, Python, and SQL. I love solving back-breaking problems and improving the stuff that makes everything tick behind the scenes.

Outside of code, I spend time working on bonsai trees. It's a slow craft, full of mistakes and opportunities for growth. I like to think it reflects how I approach development.
